BREAKTHROUGH: Controller running as of v17.

ROOT CAUSE OF HSE (all previous versions):
After HCRST, the VL805 MCU re-runs its firmware init and posts events
to its own internal ring, setting USBSTS.EINT. If RS=1 is written while
EINT=1, the MCU delivers that pending event using its stale internal ring
pointer (not our ERSTBA) → DMA to unmapped address → HSE in <1ms.
Fix: poll until EINT+HSE both clear after HCRST, then tight register
burst + RS=1 immediately. One millisecond wait was all it took.
